# Power BI Dashboard Specification

## Page 1: Executive Sales Overview

- KPI cards: Total Revenue, Gross Profit, Profit Margin, Average Order Value.
- Line chart: Monthly revenue trend.
- Bar chart: Revenue by region.
- Slicers: Region, Product Category, Date.

## Page 2: Product and Inventory Intelligence

- Bar chart: Revenue by product category.
- Bar chart: Gross profit by product category.
- Matrix: Category, orders, revenue, gross profit, margin.
- Decision focus: identify high revenue, low margin categories for inventory review.
