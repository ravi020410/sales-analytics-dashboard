# Dashboard Walkthrough

## Executive Summary Page

Start with the headline KPI cards and trend chart. The page answers whether
retail sales analytics performance is healthy, watchlisted, or off-track.

## KPI Page

Use KPI decomposition to identify which business segment, time period, or
operational driver explains the current result.

## Recommendation Page

Convert findings into a decision: what to fix, where to invest, and what to
monitor next.
